What is a Personal Loan? +
A personal loan is a type of unsecured loan that is typically used for personal expenses, such as unexpected medical bills, home repairs, or other unexpected expenses. Unlike a mortgage or car loan, personal loans do not require collateral, such as a house or car, to secure the loan. Instead, the lender typically evaluates the borrower's creditworthiness and income to determine their ability to repay the loan.
